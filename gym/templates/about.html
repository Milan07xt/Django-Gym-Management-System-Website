Our Journey

Our Story

Founded 2015

At FitZone, our journey began with a simple but powerful belief: fitness is for everyone. Founded in 2015, we set out to create a space — and a system — where people of all ages and fitness levels could thrive.

2015
The BeginningOpened our first location with just 12 equipment pieces and 3 trainers. Built the first version of our management software to track members and plans.
2017
Rapid GrowthHit 200 members milestone. Expanded the software to handle equipment tracking, enquiry management, and staff payroll.
2020
Going DigitalLaunched a fully web-based management platform. Integrated online member registration, plan selection, and CSV reporting tools.
2024
TodayServing 500+ active members, 20+ certified trainers, and continuously expanding features with the goal of making gym management effortless.
